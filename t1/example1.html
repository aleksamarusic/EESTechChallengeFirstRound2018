Gavyn Wright

Real Name:
Gavyn Wright
Profile:
British violinist, conductor, contractor and orchestra leader of The London Session Orchestra and the Penguin Cafe Orchestra. Best known for his orchestral arrangements on pop productions as well as numerous TV and movie soundtracks.

Gavin Wright And The London Film Orchestra, Gavin Wright Film Orchestra, Gavyn Wright Group, Gavyn Wright Strings, John Dankworth Paul Hart Octet, Orquesta de Gavin Wright, Penguin Cafe Orchestra, Penguin Cafe Quartet, The Astarte Session Orchestra, The London Session Orchestra, The New Symphonia
